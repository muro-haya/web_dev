Cars

Overview

The word "Cars" can refer to several things: a popular animated movie franchise by Pixar, a specific model of vehicle, or even a general term for automobiles. The "Cars" movie series, starting with the 2006 film, features anthropomorphic vehicles in a world where cars are the primary inhabitants. Cars are also fundamental modes of transportation for humans, typically four-wheeled vehicles propelled by an internal combustion engine.

Release year: 2006
